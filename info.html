How to Use

Step 1: Setup

Ensure your camera is properly positioned and well-lit. The detection area is marked with an orange box. Make sure you have a stable internet connection and the latest version of your web browser.

Step 2: Camera Access

When you first start the detection, your browser will ask for camera access. Click "Allow" to grant permission. This is necessary for the gesture detection to work.

Step 3: Hand Positioning

Position your hand within the orange detection box. Make sure your hand is clearly visible and well-lit. The system works best when your hand is centered in the box and not too close or too far from the camera.

Step 4: Gesture Recognition

Show Indian Sign Language gestures one at a time. Hold each gesture steady for a moment to allow the system to recognize it. The detected letter will appear in the prediction box.

Step 5: Using the Interface

  • Click "Start Detection" to begin gesture recognition
  • Use "Clear Letters" to reset the detected letters
  • Monitor the confidence level in the prediction box
  • View the accumulated letters in the detected letters section

Tips for Best Results

  • Ensure good lighting conditions
  • Keep your hand steady while showing gestures
  • Maintain a consistent distance from the camera
  • Use clear and distinct gestures
  • Allow the system a moment to process each gesture
