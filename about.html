About Us

Our Mission

We are dedicated to bridging the communication gap between the hearing and deaf communities through technology. Our Indian Sign Language Detection system aims to make communication more accessible and inclusive for everyone.

Our Team

Our team consists of passionate developers and researchers working together to create innovative solutions for sign language recognition. We believe in the power of technology to create positive social impact and improve accessibility.

Technology

We utilize state-of-the-art machine learning and computer vision techniques to accurately detect and interpret Indian Sign Language gestures in real-time. Our system is continuously improving to provide better recognition accuracy and user experience.

Features

  • Real-time gesture detection
  • High accuracy recognition
  • User-friendly interface
  • Continuous learning and improvement
  • Accessible design
